GENERAL MAINTENANCE

Gardens, with their vibrant colours and fresh fragrances, are delightful havens that bring joy to any home. This is what we would like our clients to feel every time they walk in their gardens. Regular garden maintenance goes a long way to making this happen. We understand that your property is a big investment, and we at Green Contracting are committed to helping you realize the dream that you have for your property. From brand-new construction to existing landscapes that need a facelift, or just a bit of maintenance, our professional staff is there to help you.

MAINTENANCE SERVICES

Green Contracting offers the following general maintenance services on a weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and every 2nd month schedule:

GENERAL CLEAN-UP

  • Raking and sweeping
  • Lawn mowing
  • Trimming edges
  • Dead-heading flowers & leaves
  • Fence clearing
  • Pruning
  • Cutting and splitting perennials
  • Garden refuse disposal
  • Turf and bed clean-up

BED CARE

  • Bed turnover
  • Weeding
  • Dividing and replanting where necessary
  • Rose care
  • Bed cultivation
  • Espalier plant maintenance
  • Outdoor pot maintenance
  • Replenishing topsoil
  • Plant training

FERTILISATION, LAWN CARE, & WEED CONTROL

  • Bed turf fertilizing
  • Lawn fertilizing
  • Lawn dressing
  • Weed control
  • Pest treatment
  • Specialised Garnum CP Fish Hydrolysate treatment
  • Grass provision through specialists

SEASONAL PLANT CARE

  • Flower change
  • Planting of seasonal plants
  • Replenishing vegetable garden
  • Pruning roses and fruit trees
  • Cutting back perennials
  • Plant fertilizer and compost

We also offer PRESSURE WASHING services, to keep your whole property looking clean and well-maintained, not just your garden.

ABOUT US

At Green Contracting, we specialise in crafting unique outdoor spaces tailored to each client's distinctive needs and preferences throughout Gauteng. Our experienced team delivers top-notch landscaping, maintenance, and specialised services that reflect your individual style and goals.

Based in Sandton, we proudly serve key metropolitan areas and districts across Gauteng, including City of Johannesburg, City of Tshwane, Ekurhuleni Metropolitan Municipality, West Rand District, and Sedibeng District. Our goal is to bring beauty and functionality to both your personal and professional outdoor spaces throughout the province.
